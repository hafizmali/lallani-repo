About Me

Lalanii Rochelle is a senior copywriter and creative consultant with a marketing and production background. She is an innovative professional with experience working with agencies crossing all industries within various CMS. Her niche writing has been seen in product reviews, descriptions, and opinion pieces. Her best work is that of a journalistic nature.

She holds a management degree with a minor in communications. She later claimed a masters degree in writing with a dual concentration in nonfiction and poetry. As a facilitator in fiction at USC, a trainer of various assistants and creative writers, and a successful freelance blogging career for fashion, beauty, entertainment and lifestyle mediums, her range of experience shows throughout her proven success rate. She is currently available contract to full-time.
